About Me

My Story

I am Riley, a UX/UI designer from Madison, Wisconsin.

I'm a UX/UI designer with a strong curiosity driven and hands on creative mindset, shaped by interests like concept art and building props from movies and TV shows. I transitioned into UX/UI design after working in customer facing and operational roles, where I saw how poor design creates frustration and inefficiency. That experience pushed me to pursue formal UX/UI training through CareerFoundry, and today I apply real world insights, problem solving, and user first thinking to design clear, intuitive digital experiences.

Design Philosophy: “Design decisions should be intentional, testable, and user driven.”
